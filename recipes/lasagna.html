Description of our Lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
